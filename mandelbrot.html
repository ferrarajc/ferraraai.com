Mandelbrot Flight Simulator

A real-time fractal explorer for iPhone, iPad, and Android, rendered on the GPU at up to 60 fps.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Mandelbrot Flight Simulator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

The app requests access to your photo library only when you explicitly tap the camera button to save a fractal screenshot. Photos are saved directly to your device and never transmitted anywhere.

On Android, the app requests READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission solely to save screenshots to your photo library. No images are read from your device.

Your fractal position, zoom level, and color scheme preferences are stored locally on your device only and never leave it.

The app does not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or data collection services.
